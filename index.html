Volunteer Services

Volunteering is a strong passion of mines. I love to give back to my community and mentor kids to aid in their educational growth! Click each organization name below to view their website and find out how you can volunteer and/or donate



Girls Who Code

Girls Who Code, GWC, is an international nonprofit organization that aims to support and increase the number of women in computer science with a mission to close the gender gap by not only preparing girls to enter the workforce - but preparing them to lead it, to improve it, and to completely and totally transform it. GWC hosts a summer Immersion Program, a specialized campus program, after-school clubs, a college club, college Loops, a series of books and code at home activities. I volunteer and host engagements at their summer Immersion Program. I am in the process of starting my own Girls Who Code club in my state with the help of GWC where I will be mentoring young girls and leading fun interactive activities

Honor Roll Foundation

Honor Roll Foundation provides high school & college students with the necessary resources as they transition to higher education by exposing them to the culture at an HBCU through a college tour and making the transition out-of-state seamless and exciting with a College Warming. They also provide College & University Support with mentorship and shadowing opportunities for college students in order to help tailor their career path. They help them see the bigger picture with their career choice and educate them on traditional and non-traditional routes that can be taken within their career. Each year I sponsor a student to attend the HBCU culture experience tour and donate supplies to the college warming. I also mentor college students in my state and help them adjust to being out of state and in college.

Chesterfield Food Bank

Chesterfield Food Bank Outreach Center is a passionate, faith-based nonprofit with a vision to nourish and strengthen the community. Their mission is about feeding those in need and empowering individuals and families to overcome the root causes of food insecurity. They recognize the unfortunate reality that many in the community fall through the gaps in aid programs offered by the county and other organizations. As a result, they have dedicated themselves to providing additional support to curb hunger for kids, families, individuals, and seniors. Chesterfield Food Bank is driven by the belief that no one should go hungry. Each year my family and I volunteer our time at Chesterfield Food Bank and help pass out food to the community, stock the selfs with food and we also donate food.
